About the Tour

Join us on a unique adventure through Porto's historic streets, where we combine the city's rich history with its charming feline residents. Our 2-hour walking tour takes you through the historic Fontainhas neighborhood, known for its colorful houses, stunning river views, and numerous street cats.

Learn about Porto's fascinating past while meeting some of its most charismatic four-legged inhabitants. Our knowledgeable guides share stories about the area's industrial heritage, cultural significance, and the special role cats have played in the community.
